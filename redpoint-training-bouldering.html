Requirements

Physical Capability:

  • Must be 18 years old or older
  • Must not be injured
  • Must have climbed a V5/5.12/6C+ in the last year
  • Must realise that this training carries a risk of Injury

Equipment Needed:

  • Climbing Shoes
  • Pull Up bar
  • Sling
  • 20mm Flat Ledge
  • 1m Length Rope
  • Scales

Assessment

At the start of your training we will put you through our specialised assessment to see where you are at In order to improve your bouldering level effectively, efficiently & as safely as possible
